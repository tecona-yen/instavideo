About

Instavideo is a minimalist, mobile-first short video scrolling platform designed to be fast, lightweight, and intentionally simple. There are no accounts, no social graph, and no clutter — just short videos and straightforward controls.

The platform is client-sided: the feed runs in your browser using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Instavideo also uses a personalisated algorithm written in JavaScript to surface videos based on your interactions, but all data and preferences are stored locally on your device. This means you can enjoy a personalised feed without any server-side tracking or data collection.

Unlike most social media platforms, Instavideo puts you in charge of the algorithm. You can adjust personalization strength, increase randomness for more variety, and manually nudge tag preferences using sliders. Your preferences are stored locally in your browser using localStorage, so you can keep control and reset or re-randomise your settings at any time.
