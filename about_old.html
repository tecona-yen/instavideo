About

Instavideo is a minimalist, mobile-first short video scrolling platform designed to be fast, lightweight, and intentionally simple. There are no accounts, no social graph, and no clutter — just short videos and straightforward controls.

The platform is client-sided: the feed runs in your browser using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Videos are served from a local ./videos/ folder, and tags are read from simple, human-readable text files. This makes it easy to host the site anywhere as a static website.

Unlike most social media, Instavideo puts you in charge of the algorithm. You can adjust personalization strength, increase randomness for more variety, and manually nudge tag preferences using sliders. Your preferences are stored locally in your browser (using cookies) so you can keep control and reset or re-randomise your settings at any time.
